Fratelli Briganti - A Local Favorite for Pasta & Pizza


Signature dish famous all around Florence: Spaghettino dei Briganti.


Nestled in Piazza Giorgini, Fratelli Briganti offers a cozy atmosphere with an open kitchen, serving up authentic pasta dishes and pizzas at affordable prices. Known for its friendly service and generous portions, it's a go-to spot for locals and visitors alike.
