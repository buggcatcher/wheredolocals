I Tarocchi - Pizzeria in Florence


Family-run, historic pizzeria


A historic spot, open since 1979 as one of the first “spaghetti & pizza” restaurants in Florence — a truly innovative concept for the late seventies.

Inspired by the theme of Tarot cards, the décor features original paintings created specifically for the space by the owner’s wife.

In the kitchen, you’ll find the owner himself cooking, and his son serving guests.

The atmosphere remains authentic and unchanged, and the pizza is exceptional — crisp, flavorful, and made with care.

Highly recommended. We suggest booking in advance.


Distance from Duomo : 1200m
