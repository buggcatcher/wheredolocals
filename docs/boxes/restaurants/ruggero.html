Homemade jarred tomato sauce, always in season.


Experience authentic Tuscan cuisine at Trattoria da Ruggero, a beloved local eatery known for its hearty dishes and warm atmosphere. Located just outside the city center, this family-run trattoria offers a menu filled with classic recipes passed down through generations.

From rich pasta dishes to succulent meats, every meal is prepared with fresh, local ingredients, capturing the true essence of Florentine cooking.
