De' Bardi Ristorante Enoteca - A Taste of Tuscany by the Arno


Traditional Tuscan cuisine in an elegant setting.


Nestled in the heart of Florence's historic center, De' Bardi Ristorante Enoteca Buchetta del Vino offers an authentic Tuscan dining experience. With a menu that celebrates regional flavors and a carefully curated wine selection, this charming eatery provides a warm and inviting atmosphere for both locals and visitors alike.

Whether you're indulging in traditional pasta dishes or savoring a glass of local wine, De' Bardi promises a memorable culinary journey in the heart of Tuscany.
