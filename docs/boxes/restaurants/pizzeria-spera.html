Pizzeria Spera


A beloved local pizza spot just a short stroll from Piazza della Vittoria and Fortezza da Basso, Pizzeria Spera is a classic Florentine pizzeria known for its delicious traditional Italian and Neapolitan-style pizzas at great value.

It's a favorite among locals and visitors alike for a relaxed meal without tourist prices and a truly authentic Italian atmosphere.
Simple, lively, and genuine — exactly how a neighborhood pizzeria in Florence should be.



What to Expect


This straightforward, no-frills pizzeria focuses on well-made pizzas with fresh ingredients, generous portions, and tasty crusts.

You'll find

  • Classic and Neapolitan-inspired pizzas
  • Choice of different dough types, selectable when ordering
  • Excellent vegetarian options — the pizza chef is vegetarian and has created many meat-free recipes, while meat options are also available
  • Casual, family-friendly atmosphere
  • Takeaway and dine-in service (takeaway prices are lower)
  • Wine, beer, soft drinks, and local craft beers
  • All pizzas are dressed with extra virgin olive oil, sea salt, and basil.
