Grotta Parri

Parri family serves just homemade lunch since 1948


La Grotta Parri is a historic Florentine trattoria located at Via Bolognese 14R, offering authentic Tuscan cuisine in a warm and welcoming atmosphere. Open for lunch from 12:00 PM to 3:00 PM, reservations are recommended.

In the evenings, the venue is available for private events. The entire space can be rented for €990, which includes seating for up to 22 guests. For each additional person beyond the initial 22, there is a charge of €45 per person.

Experience the charm of traditional Florence with hearty dishes, generous portions, and a friendly staff that makes you feel right at home.

Distance from Duomo : 2,7km
Distance from Ponte Vechhio : 3,7km
