Cibrèo Caffè (Sant'Ambrogio)

Cibrèo Caffè in Sant'Ambrogio serves as a welcoming spot for locals and visitors alike. Open from morning to midnight, it offers a relaxed environment to enjoy coffee, pastries, and light meals, reflecting the warm hospitality of the Cibrèo brand.
