Cibreo restaurant

Cibrèo is a Florentine culinary institution since 1979, offering an elegant journey through authentic Tuscan flavors. Located in the heart of Sant'Ambrogio, it delights guests with seasonal dishes crafted from local ingredients, all served in a warm, retro-inspired setting that captures the soul of Florence.
