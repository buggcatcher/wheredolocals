Caffè Amerini


A traditional Italian bar located in the heart of Florence, just a short walk from Piazza della Repubblica and Ponte alla Carraia. It's a beloved local spot that combines the timeless charm of an Italian café with a touch of modern elegance.

The interior features a well-stocked bar, skilled bartenders, and comfortable seating, making it an inviting place to relax at any time of day.
It's the perfect stop for breakfast, lunch, a sweet afternoon break, or an evening aperitivo.



Breakfast


Start your day Italian-style with excellent coffee, cappuccinos, pastries, and fresh baked goods.


Lunch (12:00 – 3:00 PM)


A daily changing menu offers simple, quality Italian dishes at fair prices.
The lunch menu is updated every day and can be found on the bar's Instagram stories.


Afternoon break


Sweet and savory snacks are available for a quick treat between sightseeing stops.


Aperitivo & Cocktails


In the evening, Caffè Amerini becomes a lively spot for cocktails.
Classics like Negroni, Martini, Gin & Tonic, plus original creations by expert bartenders.
Drinks are served with light snacks such as chips, olives, and taralli.
