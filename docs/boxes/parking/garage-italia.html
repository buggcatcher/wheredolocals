Garage Italia – Easy Parking in the Historic Center of Florence Garage Italia (Easy Parking) is located at Via Fiesolana 19,
in the historic center of Florence, just a few minutes' walk from the Duomo, Santa Croce, and the Accademia Gallery.
It is a convenient and secure solution for guests staying in the Duomo, Sant'Ambrogio, and Santa Croce areas.

Approximate walking distances: 7 minutes to the Duomo, 7 minutes to Santa Croce, 9 minutes to the Accademia Gallery, 10 minutes to Palazzo Vecchio and Piazza San Marco.




The garage is located inside the Limited Traffic Zone (ZTL).


Important:

The parking price includes license plate registration with the Municipality of Florence, allowing legal access to reach the garage without penalties.

Is the price inclusive of the ZTL traffic authorization?

Yes, the parking price includes the license plate registration for ZTL access.


How far is the Duomo?

The garage is approximately a 7-minute walk from the Duomo.


Does the car remain in this garage?

For short stays, the car remains inside the garage.
For stays longer than 48 hours, if the client confirms the car will not be used, it may be moved to other guarded garages owned by the company. Upon return, the car will be available again at the original garage.


With the daily rate, can I enter and exit freely?

No. The daily rate applies only if the car remains parked. If the vehicle is collected multiple times during the same day, the hourly rate will apply.
