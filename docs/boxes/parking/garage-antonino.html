How to Reach Garage S. Antonino (Shortest Route)

Starting point: Via Nazionale, Florence
Destination: Garage S. Antonino

  1. Drive along Via Nazionale until the end of the street.
  2. Continue onto Largo Fratelli Alinari, where you will find an electronic gate regulating access to the ZTL.
  3. ZTL access:
    If you are heading to Garage S. Antonino, do not worry about the ZTL gate.
    The garage will communicate your license plate to the Municipality of Florence, ensuring authorized access without penalties.
  4. Immediately after the gate, turn left into Piazza della Stazione and continue straight toward Piazza dell'Unità Italiana.
  5. Drive inside the square and enter Via Sant'Antonino, which leads directly to Garage S. Antonino.
