Garage Tintori


Garage Tintori in the Historic Center of Florence Garage Tintori is located in the historic center of Florence, in the Santa Croce area,
just a short walk from Piazza Santa Croce, the Arno River, and within easy reach of the Duomo and Ponte Vecchio.
It is a private, covered garage with direct ZTL authorization and a highly convenient included free shuttle service for our guests.

This service is extremely convenient for guests with luggage or apartments not within immediate walking distance.




The garage is located inside the Limited Traffic Zone (ZTL).


Important:

ZTL access is managed directly by the garage.
Your license plate will be registered with the Municipality of Florence, allowing you to reach the garage legally without requesting a separate ZTL permit.
