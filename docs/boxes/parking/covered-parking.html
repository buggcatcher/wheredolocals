Covered Parking in Florence



Official website:
https://www.fipark.com/


These parking facilities are:

  • Covered
  • Monitored
  • Paid by the hour
  • In some cases with a maximum daily rate

Note on rates


Not all parking facilities have a fixed daily rate.
Many apply an hourly rate with a daily maximum cap.
Up-to-date rates are always available on the individual parking pages on fipark.com.


The only parking facility with a €15 daily rate and electric vehicle charging (up to 20 charging points) is Parterre.
The hourly rate is €2.
https://www.fipark.com/parcheggi/parterre/

USe Bmove app for discounts !


In parking facilities managed by Firenze Parcheggi, it is possible to obtain discounted rates by using the Bmove app.


With Bmove:

  • Hourly rates are lower than standard payment rates
  • No ticket is required at entry
  • License plate recognition is automatic
  • Payment is processed automatically upon exit

Download the Bmove app (iOS / Android):
https://www.bmove.com/


How it works:

  • Register with your email address, car license plate, and payment card
  • Enter the parking facility → the barrier opens automatically
  • Exit → the amount is charged automatically

We recommend downloading and setting up the app before arrival, using Wi-Fi.


Important advantage


All parking facilities managed by Firenze Parcheggi S.p.A. have entrances and exits located outside the ZTL gates.
You can reach them freely without any special permits.


Recommended solution if you want to park safely near the city center and save money by using the Bmove app.
