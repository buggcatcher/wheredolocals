La Habana 500

Dance the night away at Habana 500 - Florence's premier Latin hotspot!

Located along the scenic Lungarno Pecori Giraldi, Habana 500 offers vibrant salsa and bachata nights under the stars.
Enjoy live music, energetic dance floors, and tropical cocktails in a lively riverside atmosphere.
Whether you're a seasoned dancer or a beginner, Habana 500 welcomes everyone to experience the rhythm of Latin America.
