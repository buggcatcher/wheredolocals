<h2>New Year's Eve in Florence</h2> 
<br> 
<p> 
Florence is getting ready to say goodbye to 2025 and welcome the New Year with a night full of music, shows, and lights.<br>
You can choose the magic of the city's squares, which will be the stars of the Capodanno Diffuso,<br>
each with its own musical vibe for an authentic and unforgettable night;<br>
dance the night away in clubs; or go for more classic events or family-friendly options for kids.<br><br>
Florence doesn't offer just a party, but a real journey through history...<br><br>
</p>

<h4>Piazza Santa Croce</h4> 
<p> 
Will host a big stage with some of the most important names in today's Italian music scene:<br>
Paola Iezzi, Tiromancino led by Federico Zampaglione, Franco126, and talents from X-Factor.<br>
The free concert starts at 9:00 pm and ends at 1:00 am, with three main moments:<br>
an opening DJ set, live performances, and a closing DJ set.<br>
Celebrating New Year's Eve in Piazza Santa Croce is perfect if you're looking for an energetic,<br>
crowd-filled atmosphere in one of Florence's most iconic squares,<br>
surrounded by music, lights, and shared celebration.<br><br>

<h4>Piazza Santissima Annunziata</h4> 
<p> 
Under the Loggiato of the Spedale degli Innocenti, the spotlight will be on choral music<br>
with "Gospel Symphony -- Florence Sings the New Year", curated by the Florence Chamber Orchestra.<br>
From 10:30 pm to 1:00 am, The Pilgrims Gospel Choir and Light Gospel Choir will perform together with the orchestra,<br>
moving from a cappella spirituals to traditional and modern gospel,<br>
all the way to pop and rock reinterpretations, in a stunning Renaissance setting.<br><br>
</p>

<h4>Piazza del Carmine</h4> 
<p> 
Will be all about jazz with "Piazza del Carmine in Jazz".<br>
From 10:30 pm to 12:45 am, the church square will host the Nico Gori Young Lions,<br>
with a special guest appearance by Fabrizio Bosso, one of the most internationally acclaimed trumpet players.<br>
Expect a night of swing and bebop, blending American tradition, original compositions, and touches of Brazilian music.<br><br>
