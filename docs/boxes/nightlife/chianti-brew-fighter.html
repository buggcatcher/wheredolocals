Chianti Brew Fighter

Local Craft Beer Pub in Florence

Chianti Brew Fighter is a pub located on Via Pisana, just outside Porta San Frediano, in a lively and characterful neighborhood that buzzes both day and night. It's a favorite among locals and sits well off the usual tourist paths.

We recommend Chianti Brew Fighter because it's a place where you can truly breathe in the spirit of the brew revolution. The owners produce their beers in Radda in Chianti, right in the heart of wine country—where they stand their ground and fight for their passion: beer.

At the pub, you'll find mostly their own beers, which, despite being craft-made, maintain high and consistent quality batch after batch. Thanks to their dedication and strong presence in the market, they also host a rotating selection of “friendly beers” from collaborators—available on tap, in bottles, and in cans.



Prices:

  • Pints on tap range from €6 to €7
  • Bottles and cans from €5 to €7
  • During Happy Hour (every day from 17:00 to 18:30):
    • Small beer €3
    • Medium beer €5

  • French fries - €5

  • Wild boar peposo braised in beer, served with cabbage and olive oil focaccia - €10
  • Cheeseburger with caramelized onion, cheddar, lettuce, and BBQ sauce - €10
  • Edoburger: quinoa cheeseburger with vegetables and smoked ricotta - €12
  • Artisan cantucci from Migliana with Vinsanto - €6


Let the bartender guide you through the tasting—always ready to recommend and explain the beers. Visit Chianti Brew Fighter to savor some of the finest local craft brews.

In addition to beer, they offer wine, cocktails, and a Tuscan menu freshly prepared in their kitchen.
