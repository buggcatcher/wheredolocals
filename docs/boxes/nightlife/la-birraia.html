La Birr'aia

La Birr'aia is the seasonal tap garden of Birrificio Oltrarno, a small agricultural brewery born on the hills of Marignolle, just above Porta Romana.

From May to October, it opens its wide outdoor space — a genuine garden surrounded by olive trees, string lights, and wooden tables — where you can enjoy craft beers made on-site and taste simple, honest, countryside-style food.

It's one of the most loved summer spots for Florentines: informal, green, cool, welcoming, and only a few minutes away from Porta Romana, on foot or by car.



Craft Beer from Marignolle

The beers served at La Birr'aia are produced by Birrificio Oltrarno using local ingredients, including hops grown right on the surrounding hills.

  • ✓ Fresh, clean Pale Ales and IPAs
  • ✓ Craft Lagers and smoother, easy-drinking styles
  • ✓ Limited-edition special releases

The beer comes directly from the brewery next door — true “zero-kilometer” craft beer.

Farmhouse-Style Street Food

  • • Tuscan boards with local products
  • • Craft burgers
  • • Seasonal hot dishes
  • • Small bites to pair with your beer

All served with the atmosphere of a real Tuscan aia (farm courtyard).


Events & Live Music

Throughout the summer season, La Birr'aia hosts live concerts, themed evenings, DJ sets, special dinners and cultural events. The wooden stage creates an intimate vibe — ideal if you're looking for something different from the usual night out.

Local Tips

  • Book if you want to have dinner
  • Arrive early for drinks — it fills up between 8:00-9:00 PM
  • Perfect after a visit to Boboli or Porta Romana
  • Check social media for updated schedules and music nights
  • Great for groups — relaxed, spacious, convivial atmosphere

How to Get There

Via di Santa Maria a Marignolle, 29/C - Florence
Just 5 minutes from Porta Romana, surrounded by greenery.

Easy to reach on foot, by scooter, by car (parking available), or via taxi/transfer.

Season & Opening Hours

Open from May to October, typically 6:00 PM - 12:00 AM. We recommend checking official channels for updated schedules and events.

Winter Tap Room

From October to April, Birrificio Oltrarno opens its underground tap room in Via delle Campora — ideal for private tastings and small group events.
