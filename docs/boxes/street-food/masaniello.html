Masaniello - The True Bufala from Battipaglia in Florence

Mozzarella di bufala is one of Campania's culinary treasures, loved even by Florentines who often travel all the way to Naples to enjoy it at its freshest.
But in Florence, finding the same quality isn't easy: it requires fast transport and careful storage.
Masaniello, located at Via Pietro Toselli 36r, succeeds thanks to a direct supply chain with Battipaglia, receiving fresh mozzarella every day.

What You'll Find at Masaniello

This is a grocery shop without tables, designed for takeaway and home enjoyment.
The atmosphere is warm, and Giacomo, the owner, is always ready to offer pairing suggestions.
If you're planning a special lunch, tell him Francesco sent you and let yourself be guided through the Campanian specialties, where "the sun makes everything taste better."



Authentic Campanian Products

Masaniello works with Masseria Fuscillo in Paestum, a historic producer of DOP-certified mozzarella di bufala.
