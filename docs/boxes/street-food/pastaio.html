I' Pastaio

Late night bakery of Campo d'Arrigo

At night, whether you're working late or coming back from dancing, before heading to bed you can enjoy a warm pastry or a tasty little pizza, fresh out of the oven. This small shop is located just outside Florence’s Campo di Marte train station, on the side near the Fiorentina football stadium.

Open from 10:30 PM to 3:00 AM, right during the hours when the bakery is working to produce sweet pastries and savory snacks to be distributed around the city, this little spot is a true Florentine experience. There’s no seating, but everyone eats together outside, right on the street.

Best reached by bike via the footbridge over the train tracks at Campo di Marte station, or by car — locals often just park right in front of the shop.
