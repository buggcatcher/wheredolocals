Tuscan Taste Wine and Beer Shop


Boutique

A boutique dedicated to artisanal wines and craft beers.
The beer selection features over 90 types of cans and bottles from Italian and European microbreweries, including a variety of sour beers.
The lineup changes constantly, offering something new with every visit.



Wine selection

The wine selection is even more extensive.
The owner personally guides customers through a curated range of bottles priced between €15 and €150.
You can either take your bottle to go or enjoy it on-site.



Enomatic & tasting system

For wine enthusiasts who appreciate perfect serving temperatures, the shop uses Enomatic automatic dispensing systems.
With a rechargeable card, you can sample a wide array of wines and pay at the end of your visit.



Available pour sizes

Available pour sizes:
30ml - 75ml - 150ml (Still wines)
50ml - 75ml - 125ml (Sparkling wines)



Enomatic configuration

The selection rotates regularly, but the shop consistently features 7 Enomatic machines with the following configurations:
8 Tuscan white wines
8 Tuscan red wines
4 sparkling wines
4 wines: 2 orange and 2 rosé (or 4 rosé)
4 Dessert wines
4 Natural wines
8 selected rotation by the owner



Number of bottles available

A total of 40 bottles are available for tasting at any given time.

Food pairing
