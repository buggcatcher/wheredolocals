Pastel Painting Workshop in an Artisan's Studio

Painting Lesson in the Heart of Florence


Join a unique painting experience with a real artist in his charming studio, nestled in the beautiful Sant’Ambrogio area of the Oltrarno—just a 15-minute walk from Ponte Vecchio.

In this 3-hour session, you'll create your own pastel artwork on a 30x40cm canvas,
guided step-by-step by the painter himself. The class is kept small, with a maximum of 6 people,
to ensure personal attention and a relaxed atmosphere.

For those looking for a deeper dive, there's also the option to join a 5-day course in oil painting,
with daily sessions: 2 hours in the morning and 2 in the afternoon.
