Drink and Paint Florence and Countryside

Experience


Flex your creative muscles as you gaze upon a beautiful vista of Florence’s rolling hills from Marignolle, just outside the city center.
In the garden of the local microbrewery La Birraia di Marignolle, you will learn how to paint the idyllic scene before you with guidance from a professional artist, while sampling beer or wine and bruschetta.
Spend an evening enjoying this beautiful natural environment in a unique way.



And after the experience...
Enjoy dinner at Birr’aia restaurant and get inspired by the unique live concert in this charming, local setting.



Included in Your Experience

3. All Painting Supplies Provided.
4. Welcome Glass of Prosecco.

6. Choice of 2 Pints of Craft Beer or ½ Liter of Wine per Guest.

- 2 Garlic, Olive Oil, Salt & Pepper Bruschetta (Bruschetta all’Aglio, Olio, Sale e Pepe).
- 2 Chicken Liver Pâté, Olive Spread, or Daily Specialty Bruschetta (choice of one).
